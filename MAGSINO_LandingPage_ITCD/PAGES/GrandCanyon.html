Grand Canyon

The Grand Canyon is a mile-deep gorge in northern Arizona. Scientists estimate the canyon may have formed 5 to 6 million years ago when the Colorado River began to cut a channel through layers of rock. Humans have inhabited the area in and around the canyon since the last Ice Age. I want to visit this because I want to experience hiking in high rocks. Grand Canyon National Park offers camping opportunities for tourists from all across the globe.
